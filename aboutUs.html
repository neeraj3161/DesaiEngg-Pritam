About Desai Engineering

Precision. Reliability. Innovation. These are the values that drive us.

Who We Are

Desai Engineering is a leading industrial fabrication and precision engineering company based in India. With over 700+ employees and a strong global footprint, we take pride in delivering high-quality metal components to clients worldwide.

Our operations span across 4 state-of-the-art plants — 3 in Pune and 1 in Pithampur (near Indore) — with a total capacity to process more than 60,000 metric tons of steel annually.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To deliver reliable, cost-effective, and innovative engineering solutions that exceed customer expectations.

Vision: To become the most trusted engineering partner across industries worldwide by leveraging advanced manufacturing and a commitment to excellence.
