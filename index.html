About Us

We are Desai Engineering, a trusted name in precision metal fabrication and industrial solutions. Based in India, our company proudly employs over 700 skilled professionals and serves clients worldwide—exporting over 30% of our production.

Operating from four state-of-the-art facilities—three in Pune and one in Pithampur (near Indore)—we have the capability to process more than 60,000 metric tons of steel annually, meeting global standards in quality and innovation.
